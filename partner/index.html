HOW THIS SHOW GETS ITS GUESTS

A cold email in 2009. A guest in 2026.

In 2009, Yohann sent a cold email to Jim Rogers. Co-founder of the Quantum Fund with George Soros. 4,200% returns in a single decade. One of the most sought-after investors alive.

Rogers replied. Most people would have asked for something immediately. Yohann didn't. He kept the conversation going. Year after year. No pitch. No ask. Just genuine connection maintained over time.

Seventeen years later, Jim sat down as the first guest on Pale Blue Nexus. The episode is live.

No agency would have kept that thread alive for 17 years. Most wouldn't last 17 weeks. This is the difference between transactional media and relationship-driven media. Pale Blue Nexus is the second kind.

The relationship is the asset. The show is the excuse to have it.

What Sponsorship Actually Buys

Most podcast sponsorships sell you exposure.
This one sells you a system.

Three things every founding sponsor walks away with, and the mechanics behind each one. Volume and access differ between Title and Main. The underlying engine is the same.

01 · Brand

Integration, not interruption

Yohann talks about you, in his own voice, as part of the show's natural flow. Your team gets a guest seat. Clips and newsletter mentions extend reach across eight distribution channels.

How it works
Host-read placements, never scripted ad copy. Logo on episode pages and end cards. Co-branded clips across YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X.
02 · Pipeline

An outbound sales engine, commission-only

Sponsorship comes with a dedicated BD function: a commission-only sales operator who prospects on your behalf. No SDR salary, no outbound infrastructure to build. You only pay when deals close.

How it works
Outreach runs on PBN's warmed-domain infrastructure. Qualified leads get handed off into your CRM. Commission structure agreed in contract, payable only on closed-won. Title gets dedicated focus. Main gets shared access.
03 · Network

The show as a closing tool

Use PBN as a mid-funnel asset for your own pipeline. Invite stuck deals or hot prospects onto the show as featured guests. Ego activates, trust deepens, and the conversation stops being a pitch.

How it works
You name the prospects. PBN extends the guest invitation under your relationship. Title sponsors get four invitations per year plus seats at The Table, Aloomii's invite-only Toronto dinner. Main sponsors get two invitations.

“We don’t sell you access to an industry. We sell you association with a host whose audience trusts him across industries.”
