About the Show

Technology is reshaping everything. Here's how to understand it.

Pale Blue Nexus is built for curious people who want to understand the forces shaping the future without having to filter through jargon, hype, or shallow takes. Every episode is a real conversation with someone who is building in, investing in, or thinking seriously about the technologies that matter.

Topics range from AI and the future of work to space tech, global markets, geopolitics, and the infrastructure of the next economy. The goal is always the same: leave the episode smarter than you started.

Hosted by Yohann Calpu, the show is supported by aloomii.com and sponsored by vibrnt.ai.
